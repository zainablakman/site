My Love for You is in my Soul

You are far, yet you are close to my soul
My eyes won't see you from afar,
But my heart feels your essence in my core.

Thinking of you gives me hope
Crying for you is a source of light
And loving you is being in heaven

I might not see you closer
But I made a promise to you, my love,
I shall cherish you now and always

I'm certain that my words won't reach you,
Yet I cast my words into the wind
through my infinite prayers and tears,
to embrace you from mighty heaven

My words I whispered to the wind
are secrets you will never hear,
only God knows how much I adore you
in the darkness of my nights.

This devotion to you, my beloved
filled my heart with contentment
, Even though you are miles away,
I only wish for you to remember:
"MY LOVE FOR YOU IS IN MY SOUL."
